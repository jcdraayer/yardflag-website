About

YardFlag is Craig Draayer's solo project exploring themes of growth, loss, joy, and weirdness.

The songs have been recorded plainly with Craig providing the acoustic and electric guitar, pedal steel, bass, and vocals recorded into a simple analog mixer, limiting the influence of technology on the work.

"It may be cliche, but god it's nice to do things simply."
